OVERVIEW

Scope, Publication Types, & FAQ

JAIBHS publishes peer-reviewed literature-based research exploring applications, implications, and future directions of AI in biomedical and health sciences.

What We Publish

  • Systematic Reviews
  • Scoping Reviews
  • Narrative Literature Reviews
  • AI-in-Healthcare Analytical Papers (ethics, implementation, bias, clinical impact)

Focus Areas

Work at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence × Medicine × Health Sciences, including:

  • AI in emergency medicine, diagnostics, and drug safety
  • AI in psychology, cognition, and mental health
  • AI in public health and epidemiology
  • AI in biomedical research and pharmacology

Who Should Submit?

  • High-school students
  • Undergraduate students
  • First-time and early-stage researchers

Authorship Guidance

Mentors/teachers are not required as authors. If a mentor designed the study, performed analysis, wrote significant sections, or contributed intellectually, they should be included as a co-author. If they only provided guidance, include them in Acknowledgments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Click a question to expand.

When is the submission deadline? ▾

JAIBHS accepts submissions year-round with rolling review. Issues are published periodically as manuscripts complete peer review and production.

Are there any fees? ▾

The $55 publication fee is requested only after acceptance. Need-based fee waivers are available, and papers are never rejected due to inability to pay.

What citation style should I use? ▾

JAIBHS accepts APA or Vancouver. All references must be complete and properly formatted.

What is the review process? ▾

Initial screening → double-blind peer review (2–3 reviewers) → editorial decision (accept / revisions / revise & resubmit / reject with feedback).

How long does review take? ▾

Timelines are being finalized. We’ll publish review-cycle targets once the first cycle begins.

What is JAIBHS’s policy on AI use? ▾

Submissions must be human-written. AI may be used for grammar, formatting, and reference organization — not to generate text, analysis, or data.

Will I receive proof/certification? ▾

Accepted authors receive an acceptance letter, a citable DOI, and a permanent journal link.

Can I submit to conferences using my JAIBHS paper? ▾

Yes — JAIBHS is designed to help students apply to conferences, undergraduate symposia, external journals, labs, and internships.

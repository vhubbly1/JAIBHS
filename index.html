Our Mission

JESM exists to help early researchers build real academic skills: critical thinking, evidence-based writing, and publication experience — using real standards and structured peer review.

  • Credible, peer-reviewed platform accessible to beginner and early-career researchers
  • Introduces students to research methodology through real academic standards
  • Promotes early research in medicine and health sciences
  • Prepares students for conferences, research programs, and future publication opportunities

JESM is an educational journal — not a competitor to top-tier medical journals — built to help students grow into confident, capable researchers.

What We Publish

Peer-reviewed, structured, literature-based research in medicine and health sciences.

  • Narrative Literature Reviews
  • Scoping Reviews
  • Systematic Reviews
  • Evidence-Based Analytical Papers

Who Should Submit

Designed for early researchers building their first academic portfolio.

  • High-school students
  • Undergraduate students
  • First-time researchers
  • Students building an academic portfolio

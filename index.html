Our Mission

JAIBHS exists to help early researchers build real academic skills: critical thinking, evidence-based writing, and publication experience — using real standards and structured peer review.

  • Credible, peer-reviewed platform for beginner and early-career researchers
  • Exposure to systematic, scoping, and narrative reviews using real academic standards (PRISMA, inclusion/exclusion, database searching)
  • Interdisciplinary work at the intersection of AI, medicine, and health sciences
  • Preparation for conferences, labs, and higher-impact journals

JAIBHS is an educational journal — not a competitor to top-tier medical journals — built to help students grow into confident, capable researchers.

What We Publish

Peer-reviewed, literature-based research exploring AI in biomedical and health sciences.

  • Systematic Reviews
  • Scoping Reviews
  • Narrative Literature Reviews
  • AI-in-Healthcare Analytical Papers

Who Should Submit

Designed for early researchers building their first academic portfolio.

  • High-school students
  • Undergraduate students
  • First-time researchers

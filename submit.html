Submit Your Manuscript

Complete the form below. You’ll then be redirected to upload your manuscript through our secure submission portal.

Submission Requirements

  • Final manuscript in .docx or .pdf
  • APA or Vancouver citation style
  • One file per submission
  • Submissions accepted year-round
  • Submit before July 15 → Fall Issue (Oct 15)
  • Submit before January 15 → Spring Issue (Apr 15). Late submissions roll into the next cycle.
  • Specific submission requirements, including format, can be found in our formatting guide (coming soon).
Mentors/teachers are not required as authors. If a mentor designed the study, performed analysis, wrote significant sections, or contributed intellectually, they should be included as a co-author. If they only provided guidance, include them in Acknowledgments.

Publication Fees

  • High School: $35 (after acceptance only)
  • Undergraduate: $55 (after acceptance only)
  • Fee waivers available upon request. Fees support DOI registration, hosting, and editorial infrastructure.

Review Process

1) Initial Screening

Scope, formatting, and completeness review.

2) Peer Review

2–3 reviewers evaluate quality and methodology.

3) Decision

Accept • Revise • Resubmit • Reject

AI tools may be used for grammar and formatting only. AI-generated content is not permitted.

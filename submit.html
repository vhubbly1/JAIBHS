Complete the form below. You’ll then be redirected to upload your manuscript through our secure submission portal.

Submission Requirements

  • Final manuscript in .docx or .pdf
  • APA or Vancouver citation style
  • One file per submission
  • Rolling review (year-round)

Publication Fee

A $55 publication fee is requested only after acceptance. No submission fees.

Review Process

1) Initial Screening

Scope, formatting, and completeness review.

2) Peer Review

2–3 reviewers evaluate quality and methodology.

3) Decision

Accept • Revise • Resubmit • Reject

AI tools may be used for grammar and formatting only. AI-generated content is not permitted.
